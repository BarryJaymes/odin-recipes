Speedy Cheeseburger Mac

Description

My son wanted to try this when he saw it on the website. It quickly became a family favorite but the best part is how quickly it comes together. You can use milk, half and half, heavy cream, really whatever you want for the dairy here.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 1/3 cups sharp chedder cheese
  1. Combine cornstarch, garlic powder, sugar, salt, paprika and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the beef and onion until no pink remainds. Drain well.
  3. Add milk, broth, and macaroni to the beef. Stir the cornstarch and spice mixture. Bring to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat and let simmer, covered for 9 - 12 minutes or until cooked. Stir occasionally and add liquid if needed.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in cheese until melted. Serve hot.
