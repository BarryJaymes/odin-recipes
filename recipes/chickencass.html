Ritzy Chicken Casserole

Description

My wife asked me to make this over the years and we just never got to it. When I finally did end up putting it together, I was shocked at how so few simple ingredients made such a delicious meal. Everyone in the family loved it enough that it's now in our regular rotation!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken legs, cooked and shredded
  • 2 cans cream of chicken or mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 box ritz crackers
  • 1 pkg broad egg noodles cooked according to pkg directions
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients except the crackers and butter.
  2. Pour into a 9x13 casserole dish, top with crushed crackers and dot with butter.
  3. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until top is crispy.
