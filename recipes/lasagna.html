Hearty Lasagna

Description

I use a lot of meat and as many cheeses as I can. Sadly after I made this a few times, nobody in the house will eat store-bought. Every time I see a new one, I try bringing it home, just for the kids to take one bite and ask when I'll be making the homemade version.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3lbs ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 cans pasta sauce
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 1 container cottage cheese
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1 egg beaten

Directions

  1. brown beef and onion on medium until no pink remains. Drain.
  2. Stir in pasta sauce and simmer for 5 minutes. Add water to thin, and seasonings to taste.
  3. combine 2 cups mozzarella cheese, with egg, parsley, garlic, ricotta and cottage cheese.
  4. spread a thin layer of sauce in pan. Top with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with 1/3 of the cheese mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat twice more. Finish with 3 noodles topped with remaining sauce.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour.
  6. Remove foil, sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and bake 15 minutes until brown and bubbly
  7. Rest at least 15 minutes before cutting.
